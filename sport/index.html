Youth

Aren't the young people of today wonderfull? I've been involved in JeKa from 1992 till 2007 and Tentenkamp from 1999 till 2006.

JeKa is a christian organisation where you start off with yongsters of 13 years old and end when they become 18. A turbulent age period.

Tentenkamp was a camp during the holidays (first week of july). It's for youngster aged 16 till 25 years old. It's organized by the youth department of the vicary. The 3 founding principles are responsibility, dialogue and "being allowed to be" [this last one is tough to translate]. I've met some of my best friends there.
